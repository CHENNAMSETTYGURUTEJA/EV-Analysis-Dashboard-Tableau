# 🚗 EV Analysis Dashboard Tableau

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) data to understand market trends, growth, and vehicle distribution.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze EV adoption over time
* Identify top manufacturers and models
* Compare BEV and PHEV vehicles
* Understand regional distribution of EVs

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

* EV growth trend by year
* Top 10 manufacturers
* State-wise EV distribution
* BEV vs PHEV comparison
* CAFV eligibility analysis

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Tableau
* Excel

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* EV adoption increased rapidly after 2020
* BEVs dominate the market
* Certain states show higher EV usage
* Top brands lead majority market share
